May 10th: Disneyland California Adventure (DCA)

Address: Buena Vista Street, Anaheim, CA 92802

Map: Interactive Map

Things to know:

  • It has two theme parks: Disneyland Park (larger) and Disney California Adventure (DCA). We'll spend Day1 at DCA and Day2 at Disneyland Park
  • We will be staying overnight at one of its hotel - Pixar Place Hotel, west of DCA (or the anaheim hotel, east of dca). Hotel check-in time is 3pm and check-out time is 11am. We can store our luggage at the Bell Desk
  • To enter the theme park, guests (age 3 or older, <3yo is free) need both valid admission and a theme park reservation for the same day and same park they want to visit
  • DCA operates from 8am-9/10pm. Disneyland Park operates from 8am-11pm. Since we're staying at the hotel, Day2 we have 30 minutes early theme park entry
  • For kid:
    • Stroller policy: must be no larger than 79 cm wide and 132 cm long, or rent one inside for $18/day
    • Baby Care Centers (changing tables, nursing areas, selling water & formula, break): Around Ghirardelli Soda Fountain
    • Kid-friendly: Disney Junior Dance Party, Monsters Inc. Mike & Sulley to the Rescue, Characters Meeting (can use App to locate)
    • Maybe: put an id tag on kid, bring blankets/jackets/umbrella, portable charger, snacks
  • If we wanna shopping, we can go Downtown Disney District
